# Project Brief: Coffee Shop Website

## Project Overview
Create a modern, responsive website for the coffee shop "Два пончика и чашка кофе" (Two Donuts and a Cup of Coffee), a specialized coffee shop offering premium coffee and homemade donuts.

## Core Requirements
- **Static Website**: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript implementation
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach with breakpoints for tablets and desktops
- **Multi-section Layout**: Hero, About, Menu, Contact sections
- **Visual Appeal**: Warm color scheme (browns, creams), high-quality images
- **User Experience**: Smooth scrolling, mobile navigation, hover effects
- **Content**: Russian language, coffee shop branding and information

## Key Goals
1. **Promote the Business**: Showcase the coffee shop's unique offerings and story
2. **Display Menu**: Highlight popular items with prices and descriptions
3. **Provide Contact Information**: Address, hours, phone, email, map integration
4. **Build Trust**: Share company history and commitment to quality
5. **Drive Engagement**: Encourage visits through compelling visuals and content

## Success Criteria
- Fully functional responsive website
- Fast loading times
- Intuitive navigation
- Professional appearance matching coffee shop branding
- Accurate business information presentation
