# Product Context: Coffee Shop Website

## Why This Project Exists
The coffee shop "Два пончика и чашка кофе" needs a professional online presence to complement its physical location. In today's digital age, customers expect to find business information online before visiting. The website serves as a digital storefront that introduces the brand, showcases offerings, and provides essential contact details.

## Problems Solved
- **Customer Discovery**: Potential customers can learn about the coffee shop's unique concept (specialty coffee + homemade donuts) and decide if it matches their preferences
- **Menu Accessibility**: Visitors can view the menu, prices, and item descriptions without needing to visit in person
- **Location Awareness**: Clear address, hours, and contact information help customers plan their visits
- **Brand Storytelling**: The website communicates the coffee shop's history, values, and commitment to quality ingredients
- **Lead Generation**: Contact form and information encourage inquiries and reservations

## How It Should Work
The website functions as an informational hub with the following user journey:
1. **Discovery**: Users land on the hero section with compelling visuals and value proposition
2. **Learning**: Navigate through About section to understand the brand story and values
3. **Exploration**: Browse the menu section to see offerings and pricing
4. **Action**: Use contact information to plan a visit or make inquiries

## User Experience Goals
- **Intuitive Navigation**: Clear menu structure with smooth scrolling between sections
- **Mobile-First Design**: Seamless experience across all devices, especially mobile users
- **Fast Loading**: Optimized images and code for quick page loads
- **Engaging Visuals**: High-quality coffee and donut images that evoke appetite and comfort
- **Trust Building**: Professional design, accurate information, and clear branding
- **Accessibility**: Readable fonts, sufficient contrast, keyboard navigation support

## Target Audience
- **Coffee Enthusiasts**: Looking for specialty coffee and unique experiences
- **Local Residents**: Seeking convenient coffee and pastry options
- **Office Workers**: Needing quick breakfast or coffee breaks
- **Families**: Wanting a comfortable place for casual meetings
- **Tourists**: Discovering local coffee culture

## Success Metrics
- Increased foot traffic from online inquiries
- Positive user feedback on design and usability
- High mobile engagement rates
- Clear conversion path from website visitor to coffee shop customer
